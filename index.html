One Planet One Earth Foundation Inc is a non-profit organization at the forefront of reforestation efforts in the Philippines. Committed to combating deforestation, environmental degradation, climate change, and global warming, the foundation operates with a vision to restore the country's forests and biodiversity. By utilizing cutting-edge technologies, strategic partnerships, and community involvement, the foundation is revolutionizing the reforestation landscape in the Philippines.
